About SK Hostel

Your comfort is our commitment

Our Journey

2015 – Founded

SK Hostel was founded to provide affordable, hygienic stays for students and travelers.

2018 – Expanded Services

Added free WiFi, kitchen access, and daily cleaning for enhanced comfort.

2020 – Safety Upgrades

Introduced CCTV, keycard access, and female-only dorms for better safety.

2024 – 10,000+ Guests

Achieved milestone of hosting 10,000+ guests from around the world.

Our Mission

To offer a home-away-from-home experience through quality, comfort, and care.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and beloved hostel in the region.
