Our Services

Comprehensive amenities and services to make your stay comfortable and memorable

Core Services

24/7 Reception

Our friendly staff is available round the clock to assist with check-in, local information, and any concerns you may have.

Luggage Storage

Free secure luggage storage for guests. Store your bags before check-in or after check-out at no extra cost.

Free WiFi

High-speed internet connection available throughout the hostel. Stay connected with friends and family or work remotely.

Daily Housekeeping

Professional cleaning service to maintain hygiene and comfort in all rooms and common areas daily.

Additional Services

Laundry Service

Self-service washing machines and dryers available 24/7. Detergent provided.

Bike Rental

Explore the city on two wheels with our well-maintained bicycles. Helmets included.

Parking

Limited secure parking spaces available for guests traveling by car.

City Tours

Join our guided city tours to discover hidden gems and local attractions.

Airport Shuttle

Convenient shuttle service to and from the airport. Advance booking required.

Event Tickets

Get discounted tickets to local events, concerts, and attractions through our partners.
